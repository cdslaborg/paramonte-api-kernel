Visualization of the benchmark output ⛓

Benchmark moral ⛓
  1. The procedures under the generic interface genEye are functions while the procedures under the generic interface getEye are subroutines. From the benchmark results, it appears that the functional interface performs less efficiently than the subroutine interface. Note that this benchmark does not even include the cost of repeated reallcations, that is, the allocation of Eye happen only once in all tests.
  2. Furthermore, the genEye() implementation with a reshape() and implicit-loop appears to be 4-16 times slower than the subroutine implementation and 2-4 times slower than the function implementation.
