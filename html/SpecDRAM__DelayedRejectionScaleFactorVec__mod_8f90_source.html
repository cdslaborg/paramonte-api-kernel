88  &the covariance matrix of the proposal distribution of "//methodname//" sampler is scaled when the Delayed Rejection (DR) &
89  &scheme is activated (by setting delayedRejectionCount>0). At each ith stage of the DR process, &

92  &The default value at all stages is 0.5^(1/ndim) = "//num2str(self%def)//", which reduces the &
