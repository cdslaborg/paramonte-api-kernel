94 "domainMaxNumCheckToStop is an integer number beyond which the program will stop globally with a fatal error message &

96 &is reset after a proposal point is accepted as a sample from within the domain of the objective function. &
97 &When out-of-domain sampling happens frequently, it is a strong indication of something fundamentally wrong in the &
98 &simulation. It is, therefore, important to closely inspect and monitor for such frequent out-of-domain samplings. &
99 &This can be done by setting domainMaxNumCheckToStop to an appropriate value determined by the user. &
