\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
{\ttfamily Sorted\+Merged\+Array} \+: The output contiguous array of the same rank, type, and kind as the input {\ttfamily Array1} whose size is the sum of the sizes of {\ttfamily Array1} and {\ttfamily Array2} and whose elements are the combined elements of {\ttfamily Array1} and {\ttfamily Array2} in ascending order.
