Use \mbox{\hyperlink{interfaceGeometryEllipsoid__mod_1_1genLogVolEllipsoid_a0259f64fa184e12041be4c60a8626fb2}{gen\+Log\+Vol\+Ellipsoid\+\_\+0D()}} when the Cholesky factorization is already precomputed and available.
