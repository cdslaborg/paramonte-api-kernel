\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item The procedures under the generic interface \mbox{\hyperlink{interfaceArrayFind__mod_1_1genReplaced}{gen\+Replaced}} are functions while the procedures under the generic interface \mbox{\hyperlink{interfaceArrayFind__mod_1_1getReplaced}{get\+Replaced}} are subroutines. From the benchmark results, it appears that the functional interface performs less efficiently than the subroutine interface. Note that this benchmark does not even include the cost of repeated reallcations, that is, the allocation of {\ttfamily Replaced} happen only once in all tests.
\item Furthermore, the {\ttfamily gen\+Replaced()} implementation with a {\ttfamily reshape()} and implicit-\/loop appears to be 4-\/16 times slower than the subroutine implementation and 2-\/4 times slower than the function implementation.
